The Sleep Loft’s Diverse Mattress Selection

Variety of Mattress Types

The Sleep Loft carries a wide variety of mattress types, making it easy for you to find the perfect fit for your personal preferences.

  • Memory Foam: Memory foam mattresses are known for their contouring properties, providing excellent support and pressure relief. Ideal for those who need relief from joint pain or who prefer a mattress that molds to their body’s shape.
  • Innerspring: A traditional choice for many, innerspring mattresses are well-known for their bounce and support. They offer a more responsive feel, with a classic coil system that many sleepers find comfortable.
  • Hybrid: Hybrid mattresses combine the best of both worlds—memory foam or latex on top for pressure relief, paired with an innerspring coil system for support. Perfect for people who want a balance of softness and firmness.
  • Latex: For eco-conscious shoppers, latex mattresses provide a natural, hypoallergenic option that’s breathable, durable, and supportive. Latex is a great choice for those who run hot while sleeping and want a cooler, more breathable mattress.
  • Adjustable Beds: For the ultimate in custom comfort, adjustable mattresses allow you to change the position of your bed with a push of a button. Whether you need to elevate your head for better breathing or raise your legs for circulation, adjustable beds offer personalized comfort.
